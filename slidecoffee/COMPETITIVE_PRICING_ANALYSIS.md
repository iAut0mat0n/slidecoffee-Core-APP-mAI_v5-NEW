## ğŸ’¡ Key Insights from Competitors

### 1. **Credit-Based Pricing is Standard** (Gamma Model)
- Free: 400 credits
- Plus: 1,000 credits/month
- Pro: 4,000 credits/month
- Ultra: 20,000 credits/month

**Insight:** Users understand credit systems. It's transparent and prevents abuse.

### 2. **Team Pricing is Per-Seat**
- Gamma Team: $20/seat
- Gamma Business: $40/seat
- Beautiful.ai Team: $40/seat

**Insight:** Per-seat pricing is expected. Charge per user, not per workspace.

### 3. **Annual Discounts are 20-30%**
- Gamma: Save up to 28% annually
- Beautiful.ai: $12/mo annual vs $45/mo monthly (73% savings!)

**Insight:** Offer aggressive annual discounts to lock in revenue.

### 4. **Premium Features Justify Higher Prices**
- API access (Gamma Pro)
- Custom domains (Gamma Pro/Ultra)
- Advanced AI models (Gamma Ultra)
- SSO & compliance (Enterprise)

**Insight:** We can charge premium for AI agent, research, brand management.

### 5. **Free Plans are Generous**
- Gamma: 400 credits at signup
- Beautiful.ai: 14-day full trial
- Canva: Robust free tier

**Insight:** Free tier should be functional enough to hook users, limited enough to convert.
